About the Coach

Turning Fear Into
Fluid Motion

Coach Amara Osei is a FINA-certified swimming instructor based in Nairobi with over 12 years of experience training swimmers of all levels — from toddlers learning water safety to competitive athletes preparing for national championships.

Having competed at the East African Games, Amara brings both professional athletic experience and a deep passion for teaching to every session at Kasarani Aquatic Center.

  • FINA Level 2 Certified Coach
  • Kenya National Swimming Team Alumni
  • Red Cross Water Safety Instructor
  • Child-friendly & inclusive approach
